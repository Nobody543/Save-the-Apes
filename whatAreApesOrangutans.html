Orangutans are one of the five kinds of Great Ape. Bornean and Sumatran Orangutans are two of the orangutans that we know most about, however, they are both critically endangered.


Here is a fact file on Bornean Orangutans:


Scientific Name: Pongo Pygmaeus

Height: 3.3-4.6 ft.

Weight: 66-200 lbs

Habitat: Lowland rainforests and tropical, swamp and mountain forests

Population: 104, 700

Fun Fact: Did you know that Bornean Orangutans are the smallest of all the orangutans.



Here is a fact file on Sumatran Orangutans:


Scientific Name: Pongo Abelii

Size: 3-5 ft.

Weight: 66-198 lbs

Habitat: Tropical and subtropical, moist broadleaf forests

Population: 14, 500
