Apes are tailless primates, however they can commonly be confused with monkeys. One big difference between the two is that monkeys have tails, whereas Apes do not.


Did you know that there are two types of Apes? The first type is the Great Ape, and the second type is the Lesser Ape. There are 5 types of Great Ape. These are Gorillas, Orangutans, Bonobos, Chimpanzees and Humans. On the other hand, there is only one type of Lesser Ape. They are called Gibbons, and are classed as a Lesser Ape because they are so different to Great Apes, however they are still Apes.


Two of the most endangered Great Apes are Mountain Gorillas and Orangutans.
