Gorillas are one of the five kinds of Great Ape. Mountain Gorillas are some of the most endangered Great Apes in the world and here is a fact file on them:


Scientific Name: Gorilla Beringei Beringei

Height: When they are standing on two feet, they are between 4 and 5 1/2 ft.

Weight: Up to 440lbs

Diet: Over 85% of their diet is made up of leaves, shoots, buds and stems.

Habitat: Hight Altitude Forests

Population: 1063
